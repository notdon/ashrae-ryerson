About Us

ASHRAE Ryerson, founded in 2014, is the Ryerson University student chapter of ASHRAE. We aim to connect students with professionals in the HVAC and building industries, giving undergraduates the opportunity to foster industry connections and learn about cutting-edge technological developments.

Our events emphasize sustainability and the environment, showcasing innovations in building technology that help to reduce energy usage or lessen the environmental impact of buildings.
