Portlands Energy Centre Tour


470 Unwin Ave, Toronto, ON

8:30 AM - 1:00 PM


Join us for a tour of the Portlands Energy Centre (PEC), a 550 MW combined cycle power generating station located in the heart of the GTA. PEC uses natural gas to run two turbines as well as steam from waste heat to run a steam turbine generator, producing safe, clean, efficient and reliable electricity for the City of Toronto. Not only will you get up close and personal with utility-sized generators, you will also meet employees from various departments at PEC and learn what it's like to have a career in the power sector. PEC is located at 470 Unwin Ave in Toronto’s Port Lands. Tour will occur on Thursday October 3rd and runs from 8:30am (sharp!) to 1pm. Please plan to arrive at PEC around 15 minutes before the tour begins.
